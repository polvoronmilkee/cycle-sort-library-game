THE CYCLIBRARIAN'S HANDBOOK

The Archive is fractured. Books are out of place, forming invisible Cycles.

  • 1. Pick Up: Click a book that is out of order. It enters your 'Hand'.
  • 2. Identify: Count how many books on the shelf are smaller than the one in your hand. That is its True Index.
  • 3. Swap: Click the book at that index. The book in your hand goes home; the displaced book enters your hand.
  • 4. Close the Loop: Repeat until you hold the book that belongs in the very first empty slot you created.

"The most efficient sort is the one with the fewest touches."
